KAVIZ — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

KAVIZ ("Teacher of the World") is a mnemonic number-encoding app developed by Kaviyarasu Thangarasu. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

1. Information We Collect

KAVIZ does not collect any personal information from users. The app works fully offline and does not require login or registration.

2. Data Storage

All data (saved words, history, notes) is stored locally on your device only using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to any server.

3. No Ads

KAVIZ does not display advertisements and does not use any ad networks or tracking technologies.

4. No Third-Party Sharing

KAVIZ does not share any data with third parties. We do not use analytics, tracking, or any external services that collect user data.

5. Internet Permission

The app may use internet connection only for the built-in AI chat feature (powered by Claude AI). No personal user data is sent — only the text you type in the chat box is processed.

6. Children's Privacy

KAVIZ is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
